Why we decided to make this ChatBot?

The natural tendency of having the things you need at a fast rate right at your fingertips exists inside all of us.
After taking a survey from our classmates and also some faculty members, it was easy to draw to a conclusion that we all face certain time lags and difficulty towards acquiring certain college documents.
Therefore, we decided to develop an interface which would be user-friendly, easy to understand and use and would deliver your document just by typing the name.
